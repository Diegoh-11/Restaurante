Wholesome Grains

Grains are small, hard, dry seeds harvested for human or animal consumption. They are a staple food worldwide and provide more food energy than any other type of crop. Whole grains are an excellent source of complex carbohydrates, dietary fiber, and several B vitamins.

Our restaurant features a variety of grain-based dishes using both whole grains and refined grains. From hearty quinoa bowls to traditional rice dishes, from whole wheat pasta to ancient grains like farro and bulgur, we offer options that are both nutritious and delicious.

We believe in the importance of incorporating whole grains into a balanced diet. Our chefs skillfully prepare grain dishes that are flavorful, satisfying, and packed with nutrients. Whether as a main course or a side dish, our grain selections complement any meal perfectly.
